Confession

You are my world
Everything I ever prayed for.

“Have you ever been Vulnerable? Have you ever tried to let yourself feel the emotion and possibly express it? Horrible isn't it?  It opens your chest and it opens up your heart and it means that someone can get inside you and mess you up.”
I read somewhere that: Love is a temporary madness, it erupts like volcanoes and then subsides. And when it subsides, you have to make a decision.” You have to work out whether your roots have so entwined together that it is inconceivable that you should ever part. Because this is what love is. Love is not breathlessness, it is not excitement, it is not the promulgation of promises of eternal passion, it is not the desire to mate every second minute of the day, it is not lying awake at night imagining that she is kissing every cranny of your body. 
Any fool can feel what I am feeling, or that’s what I thought, I never wanted this to be a passing feeling, but this feeling, oh god, this is special. Love itself is what is left over when being in love has burned away, and this is both an art and a fortunate accident.
I have had crushes, I have had infatuations, I have had seen people who cane make your heart slip a beat but I have never meet anyone whom whom you can see your life with, which whom you can see yourself growing old, with whom you can feel it’s can be a start of something special! You are that person to me. 

I remember the first time I hugged you, my heart was throbbing, I knew then and there that you are special and tumhe kabhi jane nai de sakta mai I still remember how big my smile was the night when I got home from us hanging out for the first time.
There are some people where it’s just the right time, everyone has a person where they see ending up with them, yet nothing has ever happened and nothing might ever happen but I am the fortunate one who gets to live the life with the person I have fallen in love with.
The idea of them is so perfect, yet the way life works out, they will always be just that for you, an idea but not us, we are special.

I am really good at making jokes about it, sending her those flirty little messages every now and then but there is a difference between being open and being vulnerable, I am really really good at being open but I am not really good at being vulnerable. I make stupid mistakes, have always been careless about a lot of things in life and then you came in my life. I suddenly wanted to take care of everything, wanted to care about every small detail about us, wanted to love you in the best possible way, I will work hard on it every single day, just trust me, give me a bit of time, I will be better.
I think the greatest thing that we can offer to each other is to be their peace, to be there softest and safest place. After receiving their grudges from the world, battling their chaotic mind, sadness, everything- you will have me. I will be your home.
I will always send you love, you can hold on to it whenever life is tough, whenever you are feeling empty, you will have my love, my arms around you.

I am not settling for less, I am with a women who makes my heart skip a beat everytime I see her; jab pehli baar deka the tumhe tab samajh hi nai maya the ki kya Karna hai, aaj phi vishwas nai hota kabhi kabhi.
You gave me no choice but to love you! Not sure what the future hold for us, but I want you in it, every hour every minute. I will fight, I will fight for you, I will fight for us. You ask do I believe in fate? 

No I don’t? But then again, how can this not be fate? How can two people who are introvert at heart end up having this long walk with superficial, supernatural and spiritual talks. 
Tell me, when was the last time you felt perfect? When was the last time you felt that adrenaline rush, when was the last time someone occupied your mind for so long that you felt that something special was brewing.  We had our moments, it’s on you, it’s on me, fate has done it’s part, it’s up to us to explore the realm of possibilities, a lifetime awaits for you, a lifetime awaits for me, maybe a lifetime awaits for us.

Or the fact that I might have loved you the first time I set eyes on you, right from the first time I saw you, loved you even more during our first drive, when the first time you held my hand my heart slipped a little more, it became yours a bit more and when you hugged me and asked me to stay then maybe I finally fell in love with you. We never get our forever, right? Life is finite and our life with each other is finite too but who said that we cannot find our forever in those finite pocket of time and warmth. 
We can create our forever and promise to never part ways, we will create memories with each other as if it was the last day of our lives. We will live the happiest days of our lives, we will give the best life to the peole we love in the limited time we have to call each other family.
